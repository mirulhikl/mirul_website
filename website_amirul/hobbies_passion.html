Hiking!!!

Hiking is a relatively new hobby for me to be honest. I gained a liking in this activity due to its rewarding view at the top of each hill or mountains that I have climbed.

It gave me a sense of peace and serenity after going through rugged and slippery terrain. One of the mountains I have climbed is Gunung Datuk, which is located in Rembau, Negeri Sembilan. I highly relate my passion for hiking with my life which means we have to go through hardships first before seeing the reward.

Motorcyle!!

My companion for the last 2 years.

My motorcyle has been my main transportation that I used to go the campus and everywhere. I enjoyed solo riding especially in the late evening. I usually listen to songs during my rides in my airpods so I would not get bored or sleepy.

It is a form of therapy to me at some sort. I went everywhere with my motorcycle and I am so grateful that my motorcycle is still functioning well till this day.
